Converter NodesÔÉÅ

As the name implies, these nodes convert the colors or other properties of various data (e.g. transparency) in some way.

They also split out or re-combine the different color channels that make up an image, allowing you to work on each channel independently. Various color channel arrangements are supported, including traditional RGB, HSV and HDMI formats.
